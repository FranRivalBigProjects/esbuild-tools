Frequently Asked Questions

Why is esbuild so fast?

esbuild is written in Go and optimized for parallel processing, making it significantly faster than traditional bundlers.

Does esbuild support tree shaking?

Yes, esbuild supports tree shaking for ES modules, removing unused code during bundling.

Why is my bundle still large?

Large dependencies, improper imports, or disabled minification can increase bundle size.

How can I optimize build performance?

Use caching, minimize plugins, and avoid unnecessary transformations.

Is esbuild better than webpack?

esbuild is faster, but webpack offers a more mature plugin ecosystem and flexibility.
